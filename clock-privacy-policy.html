Privacy Policy for Clock Max

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Introduction

Clock Max ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Clock Max does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local).

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally on your device to provide the extension's functionality:

  • Alarm settings - Your configured alarms, times, labels, and ringtones
  • World clock preferences - Selected time zones and display preferences
  • Stopwatch records - Lap times and session data
  • Timer configurations - Timer settings, presets, and labels
  • Todo list items - Your tasks and completion status
  • User preferences - Language, theme, sound volume, notification settings
  • Statistics data - Usage statistics for your personal productivity tracking

How We Use Your Data

All stored data is used solely to:

  • Provide core extension functionality (alarms, timers, todo list, etc.)
  • Remember your preferences across browser sessions
  • Display your personal statistics

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All data remains on your local device.

Third-Party Services

Clock Max does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions for functional purposes only:

  • alarms - Required to schedule and trigger alarms and timers
  • notifications - Required to display alarm and timer notifications
  • storage - Required to save your settings and data locally
  • offscreen - Required to play audio notifications (Manifest V3 limitation)
  • windows - Required to open notification windows for alarms

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is maintained by your browser's built-in security mechanisms. We do not have access to your data.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can export your data using the built-in export feature
  • You can modify or delete individual items within the extension

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
